# Requirements - Milestone v2.5: Hardened EKF Localization & Dynamic Sensor Fusion (Archived)

All requirements for hardening robot localization, EKF pose estimation, outlier filters, and dynamic covariance scaling under slippage, tilt, speed, and distance limits were fully completed and validated on 2026-05-18.

## Requirements

### Category: 3D Absolute Spatial Boundaries
- [x] **HARDEN-01 (3D Bounds)**: Discard AprilTag visual estimates if target coordinates fall outside the physical field perimeter (X/Y limits) or vertical expectations (negative/underground Z or high floating Z values).
  * *Outcome*: Validated in `testSpatialBoundaryRejection`. Correctly filters out-of-bounds coordinates.

### Category: High-Speed Dynamics Vision Protection
- [x] **HARDEN-02 (Motion Blur Lockout)**: Ignore visual measurements when the robot's instantaneous angular velocity (measured by Pigeon 2 / Pinpoint gyroscope) exceeds $2.0\text{ rad/s}$ ($115^{\circ}/\text{s}$) to avoid blurred frames.
  * *Outcome*: Validated in `testAngularVelocityBlurRejection`. Rejects frames above $2.0\text{ rad/s}$ threshold.
- [x] **HARDEN-03 (Collision Shock Guard)**: Reject camera measurements when linear acceleration exceeds a safe shock threshold ($2.5\text{ G}$), indicating a collision or vibration that degrades perspective transform math.
  * *Outcome*: Validated in `testCollisionShockLockout`. Initiates a multi-frame lockout period.

### Category: Odometry Tilt & Beaching Safety
- [x] **HARDEN-04 (Tilt Protection)**: Monitor IMU pitch and roll angles. Dynamically scale up the wheel odometry state covariance $Q$ by $100\times$ when absolute tilt exceeds $8^{\circ}$ (slippage), and fully freeze encoder odometry inputs if tilt exceeds $15^{\circ}$ (beached wheels).
  * *Outcome*: Validated in `testOdometryCovarianceScalingUnderTilt` and `testOdometryFreezeUnderBeaching`. Scales $Q$ matrix correctly and halts pose updates under high tilt.

### Category: Elite Distance & Multi-Tag Scaling
- [x] **HARDEN-05 (Distance Noise Scaling)**: Dynamically scale measurement noise standard deviation $R$ quadratically with distance $d$ from the camera to the tag to trust distant observations less.
  * *Outcome*: Validated in `testVisionDistancePenalization`. Multiplies covariance values quadratically according to AprilTag coordinates.
- [x] **HARDEN-06 (Multi-Tag Multiplier)**: Scale down standard deviations (increasing measurement trust weight) by a factor of $\frac{1}{\sqrt{\text{numTags}}}$ when multiple tags are visible in a single sensor frame.
  * *Outcome*: Validated in `testVisionMultiTagScaling`. Correctly applies standard deviation divisor factor.
