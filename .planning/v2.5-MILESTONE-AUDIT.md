#### **HARDEN-04 (Tilt Protection)**: Monitor IMU pitch and roll angles. Dynamically scale up the wheel odometry state covariance $Q$ by $100\times$ when absolute tilt exceeds $8^{\circ}$ (slippage), and fully freeze encoder odometry inputs if tilt exceeds $15^{\circ}$ (beached wheels).
- *Status*: **PASSED**
- *Evidence*: Implemented in `PoseEstimator.kt` ([PoseEstimator.kt](file:///c:/Users/david/dev/robotics/ftc/ARESLib-Kotlin/core/src/main/kotlin/com/areslib/math/PoseEstimator.kt)).
  - **Slippage Compensation**: When tilt is $> 8.0^{\circ}$, the EKF scales the process noise covariance matrix $Q$ by $100\times$ to de-weight slipping wheel inputs. Verified by `testOdometryCovarianceScalingUnderTilt` in [PoseEstimatorTest.kt](file:///c:/Users/david/dev/robotics/ftc/ARESLib-Kotlin/core/src/test/kotlin/com/areslib/math/PoseEstimatorTest.kt).
  - **Beaching Safe Freeze**: When tilt is $> 15.0^{\circ}$, encoder delta inputs are completely frozen to prevent pose drift. Verified by `testOdometryFreezeUnderBeaching` in [PoseEstimatorTest.kt](file:///c:/Users/david/dev/robotics/ftc/ARESLib-Kotlin/core/src/test/kotlin/com/areslib/math/PoseEstimatorTest.kt).
